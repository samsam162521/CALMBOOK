Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about CalmBook

Is CalmBook confidential?

Yes. All sessions are private and encrypted. Your information is protected and we follow strict confidentiality standards to ensure your privacy.

Are the counselors licensed?

Yes. Every counselor on our platform is verified, licensed, and experienced in their respective field of mental health support.

Can I change my counselor?

Yes. You can request to connect with a different counselor anytime if you feel it would better suit your needs.

Is online counseling effective?

Research shows that online counseling is as effective as in-person therapy for many conditions, offering convenience and accessibility.

How do I book a session?

Simply log in to your account, browse available counselors, and select a time slot that works best for your schedule.
